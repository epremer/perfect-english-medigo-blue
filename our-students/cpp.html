College Prep Program

Our Students / College Prep Program (CPP)

What is the Perfect English College Prep Program?


Our signature program at PE is the College Prep Program – CPP. Designed in house by our College Director, it equips students who will study abroad not only to enhance their language fluency but to learn college skills. The program was designed on a TOEFL-IELTS platform with a focus on: vocabulary, oral, writing, reading, and listening skills. However, it goes much deeper into each skill to help students understand how these skills work at a college level.


Students spend 2 years in the program learning each skill and then they can opt to take a fluency test (TOEFL or IELTS or even Duolingo). The unique aspect of CPP is we also offer college advising and assistance to qualified students. We work with many community colleges and their “Pathway” programs to 4-year colleges. A student can complete 2 years at a community college and then transfer to complete their final 2 years of a BA degree at any university in the Pathway program. Students who choose this option can often skip the fluency testing because PE is authorized to assist students to enroll in English 1A and if they pass the course they are accepted into the college program without a fluency exam or any additional ESL coursework. This can save a student time and money. We are the ONLY school in Changchun that offers this program.


In the future we hope to offer Chinese students the opportunity to complete 2 years of college at an accredited US community college while remaining in China. When we launch that program it will be the only one of its kind in China!


At PE we believe education is the key to success. We want our students to have access to all the keys we can offer them.

Interested in teaching English in China? Go ahead and reach out on our contact page. We'll be pleased to answer you within 24 hours.
