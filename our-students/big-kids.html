Our Older Students 7-12 years


Our primary school age children are the heart of what we do. Unlike the younger children this group is more engaging. They want to play and have fun, but they also need to learn. Our teachers must find the perfect balance of playing and learning to keep these students engaged. This is the group that will need to test in 6th grade in order to get into a top-rated middle school, so learning English can mean higher test scores. Many of our students score top marks on the English portion of their exam, this speaks highly to their ability, and to the quality of how we teach.

Interested in teaching English in China? Go ahead and reach out on our contact page. We'll be pleased to answer you within 24 hours.
