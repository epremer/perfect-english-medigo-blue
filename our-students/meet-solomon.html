Meet Our Students: Solomon


Here is an example of one of these top students, meet Solomon (Zhou Le) his story is unique in some ways but fairly typical of many Chinese kids. Solomon had been a pretty good student until he went to middle school. There he was with other top students but he found himself struggling. When it was time to take the exam for entering high school he didn’t do well. He managed to get into a good school but only because he could play the viola. In high school he continued to struggle and finally decided to leave the Chinese educational system. He opted to attend a private high school but the level of education wasn’t good. Finally, he came to Perfect English and joined our College Prep Program. In one year, he managed to improve his English to the point where he took English 1A (on-line) from a Community College in the US and passed with the highest score in the class. He then enrolled at College of Alameda to complete a 2-year program and attain his Associate Science degree in Business Management. He is about to transfer to complete his Bachelor’s degree in Business. He went from failing in the Chinese system to thriving in the “western” system of education.
