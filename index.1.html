Things to Do While Teaching in Changchun

Have your own great experiences while creating a great experience for our students.

Changchun has a rich history and there are many places in the city where you can still see its past.

Changchun has “bullet” trains that can connect the largest cities in China so travel times are greatly reduced.

Changchun is home to China’s movie industry, the “Hollywood” of China, the city’s roots in making films goes back to 1940.

Interested in teaching English in China? Go ahead and reach out on our contact page. We'll be pleased to answer you within a few hours.
