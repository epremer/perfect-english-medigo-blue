Our Teachers / Why They Teach

Why they teach for Perfect English

Our teachers come from many places and with their own reasons for teaching in China. Mostly they come teach in China for these reasons:

  • China is one of the most vibrant emerging economies in the world today. Life here is very exciting as this country grows.
  • Teaching in China means: influencing a new generation of students who are going abroad to further their education, in numbers larger than ever before.
  • Chinese is quickly becoming a language of a growing global community. Changchun is the home of Mandarin Chinese and the best place to learn the Chinese language.
  • Teaching abroad offers flexibility, and being in China offers a unique experience to learn a new culture by living in it.

These are just a few reasons why our teachers come to China. Why they decided to teach for PE, is simple:

  • We offer a western style of management so our contracts are simple and clear.
  • We offer a pleasant working environment (25 teaching hours) with free time to do other things.
  • We encourage professional growth and even offer scholarships for continuing education.
  • We offer assistance in daily living tasks that require Chinese language.
  • We take care of all government paperwork and we guarantee your working visa will be legal so you never have to worry.
  • We listen to you and we work as a team, we look for solutions together.

Become a part of our team, contact us to find out how.




Spotlight On Our Teachers:

Carien


My name is Carien, and I am from South Africa, where I worked as a project manager. It was always my dream to work with children though and so when I met someone who was teaching in China, I realized that by teaching English I could realize my dream. I chose China because it offered more teaching opportunities and I could also learn about a culture which I knew very little. I started teaching English in April 2014, in Changchun, and have been teaching at Perfect English since December 2016.

Living and working in China can be challenging at times due to the language barrier and the huge cultural differences. It takes time to understand the Chinese norms and behaviors and this can potentially be overwhelming. Fortunately, I made friends with other foreigners who became like family to me. Their love and support has carried me through the more difficult times. These friends are from all over the world and I enjoyed learning about their respective home countries and cultures, which has given me a global perspective of life.

I love teaching children here, it gives me so much joy, and we have lots of fun while learning. Building relationships with my students and their parents can sometimes be challenging, but it’s also satisfying when you manage to establish those relationships. Currently I have 90 students between the ages of 4-12, seeing them grow and flourish and gaining confidence in my classroom has been very rewarding.

This job has helped me to discover my life’s passion, which is teaching and helping children to learn, grow, and reach their full potential. To further enable me to achieve this; I started working on my Master’s degree in Psychology with an emphasis on child and adolescent development and leadership as my majors. I’m constantly doing courses to enhance and develop my knowledge and abilities which help me to better understand how children learn and develop.

Perfect English also supports my quests for my own personal development. They provide a flexible schedule, good salary, and a low cost of living, so I am able to live a comfortable life while still paying for my studies, and even travelling to other countries during my vacations.

Interested in teaching English in China? Go ahead and reach out on our contact page. We'll be pleased to answer you within 24 hours.
