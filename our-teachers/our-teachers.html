Our Teachers / Who They Are

Who They Are

Perfect English (PE) is primarily an after-school English program. Our teachers instruct students (ages 3 to 12) at our branch schools and teach older students at some local middle and high schools. We also teach at a few colleges, therefore our teachers need to be flexible.


Our current staff of nearly 30 foreign teachers includes a mix of countries. We have foreign teachers from the UK, Canada, the US, and South Africa. We are always looking to add staff because our branches keep growing. A majority of our foreign teachers have been with us over 3 years and some even longer. One reason is that we encourage the professional growth of our teaching staff. We offer training and help to find continuing educational courses for them to grow as professionals. At PE we stand behind our motto “Quality Education First.”
